# Related Projects

The following repositories are part of the same research programme — applying rigorous, evidence-based frameworks to the intersection of AI, regulation, energy, and financial risk.

---

## [Sanctions & Export Controls — AI and Oil](https://felipelago17.github.io/Sanctions-and-Export-Controls-AI-and-Oil/)

Analysis of how sanctions regimes and export-control frameworks apply to AI technology and energy commodities. Examines dual-use classifications, enforcement trends, and compliance obligations for organisations operating across jurisdictions.

---

## [AI Regulatory Monitor](https://felipelago17.github.io/AI-regulatory-monitor/)

A structured tracker of AI regulatory developments globally — legislation, guidance, enforcement actions, and standards. Covers the EU AI Act, US executive orders, UK and APAC frameworks, and sector-specific rules.

---

## [Responsible AI Evaluation](https://felipelago17.github.io/Responsible-AI-evaluation/)

Frameworks and methodologies for evaluating AI systems against responsible-AI principles: fairness, transparency, accountability, safety, and human oversight. Bridges academic research and operational implementation.

---

## [Joint Ventures & the Energy Trilemma](https://felipelago17.github.io/Joint-ventures-and-Energy-Trilemma-/)

Examination of joint-venture structures in the energy sector through the lens of the energy trilemma — balancing security of supply, affordability, and sustainability. Considers how AI-driven optimisation affects each dimension.

---

These projects share a common methodological commitment: claims are tied to evidence, uncertainty is stated explicitly, and the goal is to improve decisions rather than to produce confident-looking outputs.
