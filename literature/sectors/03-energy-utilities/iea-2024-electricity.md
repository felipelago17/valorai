## Summary

The IEA's annual electricity analysis and forecast highlighted the growing contribution of data centres to global electricity demand, with AI workloads identified as a significant demand driver alongside cryptocurrency mining and cloud computing more broadly. The report projects continued demand growth through the forecast horizon, with implications for electricity pricing, grid reliability, and the carbon intensity of AI infrastructure in markets where marginal generation remains fossil-fuel-based.
