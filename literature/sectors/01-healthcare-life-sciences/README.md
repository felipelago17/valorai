# Sector 01 — Healthcare & Life Sciences

**Value questions**
1. Does AI-assisted diagnosis improve accuracy against clinician-alone baselines, and at what cost per case?
2. What is the cost-per-QALY or time-to-market reduction from AI in drug discovery and clinical trials?

**Cost questions**
1. How does automation bias affect diagnostic error rates, and what is the resulting liability exposure?
2. What are the regulatory compliance costs (FDA SaMD, EU MDR) of qualifying and monitoring AI clinical tools?
3. How does AI-enabled deskilling erode the clinical judgement required as a safety backstop?
