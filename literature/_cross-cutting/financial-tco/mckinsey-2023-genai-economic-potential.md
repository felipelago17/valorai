## Summary

McKinsey Global Institute surveyed dozens of AI use cases across industries and estimated the aggregate annual potential of generative AI at $2.6–4.4 trillion. Value is heavily concentrated: four functional domains (customer operations, marketing and sales, software engineering, and R&D) account for the majority of the potential. Banking, technology, and life sciences show the highest sector-level potential. The report treats these as gross potential figures rather than realised returns and acknowledges significant implementation variance.

## What it lets valorai measure

Sets the optimistic ceiling for AI value in sector-level business cases. Any sector projection in valorai's framework should be checked against this sizing to assess whether the estimate is within plausible bounds.

## Caveats / limits

Industry-report methodology; McKinsey is both consultant and publisher, creating incentive alignment toward optimistic framing. Figures are potential estimates, not observed outcomes. The MIT NANDA (2025) reality-check source directly challenges whether enterprise AI spend realises anything close to this potential.
