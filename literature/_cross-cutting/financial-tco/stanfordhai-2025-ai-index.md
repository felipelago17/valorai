## Summary

Stanford HAI's annual index compiles metrics across AI research, adoption, regulation, and economics. Particularly relevant for valorai are the inference-cost-per-token trajectory (which affects the financial-TCO calculation as deployment scales) and the rising training-compute and energy demands of frontier models (which affect both financial and environmental cost). The report is multicontributor and methodologically transparent.

## What it lets valorai measure

Enables trend-adjusted TCO modelling: as inference cost falls, the financial-TCO calculation must be updated annually. Grounds energy-cost projections in empirical compute-demand trends.

## Caveats / limits

Descriptive rather than causal; benchmarking methodology varies year-on-year, making longitudinal comparison imperfect. Does not directly measure enterprise deployment outcomes.
