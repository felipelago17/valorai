# Cross-Cutting Lens: Trust, Accountability & Liability

This lens captures the costs arising from opacity, unexplainability, and contested accountability in AI decision-making: regulatory compliance with explainability obligations (EU AI Act, sector-specific rules), litigation exposure from AI-assisted decisions, audit-trail requirements, and the organisational cost of managing trust when AI errors have public consequences.

**Scoring across sectors:** Identify decisions made with AI assistance that are subject to due-process or explanation obligations. Estimate legal and regulatory cost of compliance and remediation. Provision for reputational cost where AI errors are attributable.
